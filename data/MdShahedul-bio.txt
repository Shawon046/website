Md Shahedul Haque is a second year PhD student in the Computer Science department at Virginia Tech. He is a full time graduate research assistant in Complex Networks and Security Research (CNSR) Lab where he works on networking, wireless, and cyber security. He received a BSc in Computer Science from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in 2021, where he was advised by Dr. A.K.M. Ashikur Rahman.
